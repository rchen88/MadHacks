About EduLearn

It is a platform for an online knowledge-based community for UW-Madison. It solves the problem of UW-Madison students trying to find an active community by combining online forums with the idea of a decentralized autonomous organization (DAO).

This is a subscription-based website that users pay 10 dollars each month to get access to the website.

Voting and token exchange is the main concept of the DAO community. Voting is a way for users to contribute to and participate in the community. It is also the key to decentralization because all the change and earning is decided by all of the members in the community. Users are also rewarded by the quality of their answers, which leads to overall forum quality improvement. The 1 token bonus of vote is designed to reward the user for their participation. The reason for paying tokens to post questions is to encourage active response and token exchange.
